# HR Analytics & Employee Attrition Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing employee data to understand workforce trends, attrition patterns, and key factors influencing employee retention. The objective was to transform raw HR data into meaningful insights using data cleaning techniques and interactive Power BI visualizations to support data-driven HR decision-making.

## Business Objective

The goal of this project was to analyze employee attrition, identify high-risk employee groups, understand workforce demographics, and uncover patterns related to job roles, salary levels, age groups, education fields, and years at the company.

## Project Workflow

### Data Preparation

* Imported raw HR employee data into Power BI.
* Used Power Query for:

  * Removing duplicate records
  * Filtering unnecessary rows
  * Cleaning and transforming data
  * Preparing data for analysis

### Data Analysis

Performed analysis on:

* Employee Attrition Rate
* Department Performance
* Job Role Analysis
* Employee Age Groups
* Education Field Analysis
* Salary Slab Analysis
* Years at Company
* Workforce Demographics

### Dashboard Development

Built an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard including:

* Employee Count KPI
* Attrition Count
* Attrition Rate %
* Average Monthly Income
* Average Employee Age
* Attrition by Age Group
* Attrition by Education Field
* Attrition by Job Role
* Salary-based Attrition Analysis
* Department-level Insights

## Key Insights

* Analyzed workforce data of **1,470+ employees**.
* Identified an overall attrition rate of approximately **16%**.
* Found higher attrition concentration among specific job roles and salary groups.
* Analyzed employee retention patterns based on age, education, department, and experience.
* Identified factors contributing to employee turnover and potential areas for HR improvement.

## Business Recommendations

* Focus retention strategies on high-attrition job roles.
* Improve employee engagement programs for early-career employees.
* Review compensation strategies for lower salary groups.
* Monitor departments with higher employee turnover.
* Use predictive HR analytics to proactively identify retention risks.

## Business Value
This project demonstrates how HR data can be transformed into actionable insights through data cleaning, analysis, and visualization. The dashboard helps organizations understand employee behavior, reduce attrition risks, and make informed workforce management decisions.
